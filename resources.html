💎 Resources#

📚 Recommended Textbooks#

  • Peter Corke, Robotics, Vision, and Control, Springer, 1st & 2nd ed., free 1st ed pdf; easy to read but sometimes skips details.

  • Craig, Introduction to Robotics: Mehcanics and Control. Pearson, 4th ed.; most popular textbook and easy to read.

  • Spong, Hutchinson, & Viidyasagar, Robot Modeling and Control; alternative to Craig’s book, more in-depth math, covers a great deal of contents not covered by Craig.

  • Murray, Li, & Sastry, A Mathematical Introduction to Robotics Manipualtion, free pdf; must-have book for roboticists, extremeley hard to read. don’t read it if you are a biginner - you will hate robotics, UC Berkeley EE106A/206A

➕✖️ Linear Algebra#

  • Introductioin to Linear Algebra by Gilbert Strang - Best Linear Algebra textbook in the world!!

  • G. Strang’s phenomenal lecture videos at MIT

  • You can also find many of his lecture videos in YouTube

  • If you want to go to graduate school and study robotics, computer vision, information system, machine learning, artificial intelligence, or data science, you should have a strong background in linear algebra.
